Grant Overview

Santa Monica College's EEO Innovative Best Practices grant focuses on strengthening equal employment opportunity, promoting workforce diversity, and increasing engagement and belonging through targeted initiatives.

The grant supports four primary activity areas: Employee Resource Groups (ERGs), mentorship programs, professional development workshops, and conference participation—all designed to create measurable improvements in retention, satisfaction, and diversity metrics across the institution.

Goals & Objectives

Goal 1: Strengthen EEO & Workforce Diversity

  • Launch and sustain Employee Resource Groups (ERGs) to foster belonging and engagement
  • Establish structured mentorship programs pairing experienced faculty/staff with newer hires
  • Deliver targeted professional development workshops on EEO, diversity, and inclusive practices
  • Support conference attendance to build knowledge and network connections

Goal 2: Measurable Outcomes

  • Number of ERG events held and total participants reached
  • Active mentor/mentee pairs and program completion rates
  • Professional development workshop attendance and satisfaction scores
  • Retention and diversity metrics tracked over the grant period
